# Things to know before you play

Honest notes on what Archean Rise does, what it doesn't, and what will surprise you. Short reads.

- **[The world is much taller](world-height.md)** — you must pick the world type when you create the
  world, deep rock is mostly cave-free, diamond is at the very bottom, and big chunks cost more to
  generate.
- **[Playing with other mods](mod-compatibility.md)** — biome mods work from 0.3.16; a couple of mods
  don't. *(Full list: [docs/COMPATIBILITY.md](../docs/COMPATIBILITY.md).)*
- **[Villages and structures](structure-grading.md)** — villages are rarer on this terrain, on purpose,
  and structures are spread further apart.
- **[Biome borders are sharp](biome-borders.md)** — that's how Minecraft works; here's what can be
  softened.

Found something that isn't here? Please tell us — that's how this list gets better.
